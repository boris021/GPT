Types of Data We Collect

Personal Information

When you register an account with Laser247, we collect personal information necessary to provide our services and comply with legal requirements. This information includes:

  • Identity Information: Full name, date of birth, gender, and nationality
  • Contact Details: Email address, phone number, and residential address
  • Account Information: Username, password, and security questions
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, payment method information, and transaction history
  • Verification Documents: Government-issued ID, address proof, and other documents required for account verification

Technical and Usage Data

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use our platform to improve our services and ensure security:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, games played, and betting patterns
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address
  • Performance Data: Site performance metrics and error reports

Communication Data

We maintain records of communications between you and our customer support team, including:

  • Chat transcripts and email correspondence
  • Phone call recordings for quality assurance and training purposes
  • Feedback and survey responses
  • Marketing preferences and communication history

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies that are necessary for the basic functionality of our website. These cookies cannot be disabled and include:

  • Session Cookies: Maintain your login session and account security
  • Security Cookies: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings

Analytics and Performance Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our website and improve our services:

  • Website usage statistics and user behavior analysis
  • Performance monitoring and error tracking
  • A/B testing for website optimization
  • Traffic source identification and conversion tracking

Marketing and Advertising Cookies

With your consent, we may use marketing cookies for:

  • Personalized advertising and promotional content
  • Retargeting campaigns on third-party websites
  • Social media integration and sharing features
  • Marketing campaign effectiveness measurement

Cookie Management

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie preference center. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and services.

Data Protection and Security Measures

Technical Safeguards

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is protected using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Personal data is stored on secure servers with access controls
  • Firewalls: Advanced firewall systems protect against unauthorized access
  • Regular Security Audits: Independent security assessments and penetration testing
  • Data Backup: Regular encrypted backups ensure data recovery capabilities

Administrative Safeguards

Our organizational security measures include:

  • Strict access controls and employee authentication procedures
  • Regular staff training on data protection and privacy practices
  • Comprehensive data handling policies and procedures
  • Incident response procedures for potential security breaches
  • Regular compliance monitoring and internal audits

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Our retention periods vary based on the type of data:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for 7 years after closure
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years for compliance and audit purposes
  • Communication Records: Retained for 3 years for quality assurance
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you withdraw consent or for 2 years of inactivity
